Hey there, I’m Yanni Lu

a multitalented UX designer and I always believe design and creativity can bring boundless potential to elevate the human experience.

I had worked as a graphic designer for 5+ years as well as a photographer and painter. Since school, I always have a passion for digital product design, I love its integration of utility and beauty. That passion inspired me to expand my career with user experience design. From my previous experience, I helped 150+ companies, included start-ups and well-established companies to promote and market their unique brands. The industry included education, real estate, food, bank, law firms, health products, cosmetics, industrial products, pets, logistics, IT, and others. I had accustomed to performing in deadline-driven environments, as well as exceptional collaborative, interpersonal, and communication skills. I work individually for the most time, and also with other skilled professionals. In my free time, I enjoy drawing, making macrame, and playing multiple musical instruments. I am always on my way to become a better artist.
